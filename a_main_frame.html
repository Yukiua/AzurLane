What is Azur Lane?

Azur Lane is a Chinese mobile game, developed by Manjuu and Yongshi, published by BiliBili. It is a side-scrolling shoot'em up simulation RPG, which includes ships from real navies like the United States Navy, Royal Navy etc., anthropomorphized into moe Anime shipgirls. The protagonists of the game is generally referred to American destroyer USS Laffey, British destroyer HMS Javelin, German destroyer KMS Z23 and Japanese destroyer IJN Ayanami, with the last two interchangeable with Global and CN/JP respectively. The Players of the game is referred to as 'Shikikan', which is Commander is Japanese. Servers went up in CN on 25 May 2017 and 2 June 2017 for iOS and Android respectively. JP servers opened on 14 September for both iOS and Android, while Global servers opened for Open Beta on 16 August 2018, officially released on 20 May 2019 for both iOS and Android.
